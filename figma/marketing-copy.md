**City of Abbottville Launches REcycle/REuse**

**A New Municipal Initiative Advancing Sustainability, Waste Reduction, and Community Solidarity**

The City of Abbottville is proud to announce the launch of **REcycle/REuse**, a forward-looking municipal initiative designed to reduce landfill waste, promote ecological responsibility, and strengthen local social solidarity.

**REcycle/REuse** brings together two complementary components on a single site: a modern recycling drop-off point and a **5,000-square-foot warehouse dedicated to the repurposing of reusable goods**. Through this integrated approach, Abbottville is reinforcing its commitment to sustainable resource management while encouraging residents to actively participate in the circular economy.

The facility will accept a wide range of items, including **furniture, electronics, household goods, and clothing**, diverting usable materials away from landfill and extending their lifecycle through repair, redistribution, and creative reuse. Items collected through the program will be evaluated for refurbishment or repurposing, ensuring that functional goods continue to serve the community.

This initiative reflects the City's long-term environmental objectives by significantly reducing waste volume while fostering greater public awareness of responsible consumption. By emphasizing reuse alongside recycling, **REcycle/REuse** moves beyond traditional waste management toward a more sustainable and resource-efficient model.

Equally important, the initiative supports **local social solidarity**. Repurposed items will be made available through community partners, social organizations, and local initiatives, helping ensure that recovered goods benefit residents while supporting inclusive and resilient community networks.

"REcycle/REuse represents a practical and meaningful step toward a more sustainable Abbottville," said the City in a statement. "By combining environmental stewardship with social responsibility, this initiative empowers residents to take part in building a cleaner, more connected, and more resilient community."

Residents are encouraged to take advantage of the new drop-off facility and to consider reuse as a first choice before disposal. Further details regarding operating hours, accepted materials, and community partnerships will be communicated by the City in the coming weeks.

With **REcycle/REuse**, the City of Abbottville reaffirms its leadership in environmental innovation and its dedication to a sustainable future - one where resources are valued, waste is minimized, and community well-being comes first.
